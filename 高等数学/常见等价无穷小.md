# 常见等价无穷小

当 $ x \to 0 $ 时

$$
\sin{x} \backsim x
$$

$$
\tan{x} \backsim x
$$

$$
\arctan{x} \backsim x
$$

$$
\arcsin{x} \backsim x
$$

$$
\sec{x} - 1 \backsim \frac{x^2}{2}
$$

$$
1 - \cos{x} \backsim \frac{x^2}{2}
$$

$$
(1+\beta x)^{\alpha} - 1 \backsim \alpha \beta x
$$

$$
(1 + x)^{\alpha} - 1 \backsim \alpha x
$$

$$
\log_{a}{(1+x)} \backsim \frac{x}{\ln {a}} ( a > 0, a \ne 1 )
$$

$$
\ln{(1+x)} \backsim x
$$

$$
e^x - 1 \backsim x
$$

$$
a^x - 1 \backsim x\ln{a} ( a > 0, a \ne 1 )
$$
